伝記

It always boggles me how we tend to create biographies of ourselves based on whatever is that we feel it’s worth mentioning, it’s like we are trying to please and validate our existence to other human beings.

My ego enjoys being flattered, but without the ego, there is no creation, only the illusion of impermanence So enough with the validation. I was named Guillermo after a lineage of 4 other Guillermo’s before me lol. I was born in Mexico City and raised in San Francisco, CA. My quest for the mystic arts began at an early age.

Did the whole film school thing, graphic design degree and all that in the establishment, but what it truly allowed me to be my true self was my fascination for ancient and mystical places and the search for the unobtainable. Many years I find myself living in Tokyo, crafting what i would refer to sacred design, photography, and film.Regards,
